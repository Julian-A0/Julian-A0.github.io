## Overview

All components included are listed in the minimum quantity of which they must be ordered. Components such as the CFSensor pressure sensor must be ordered in a minimum of 5. Other components such as the Microchip MCP6004, Phihong 15V DC supply adapter and Texas Instruments LM7805 voltage regulator can be acquired in singular quantities.
